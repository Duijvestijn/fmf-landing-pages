Frequently asked
questions

Does a Reservation of Interest obligate me to invest?

No. A Reservation of Interest is entirely non-binding. It simply secures your place in the queue and triggers our investor relations team to send you the full Private Placement Memorandum. You make no financial commitment until you have reviewed all offering documents and signed a subscription agreement.

Who qualifies as an accredited investor?

Under SEC Rule 501, an accredited investor is an individual with a net worth exceeding $1,000,000 (excluding primary residence), or annual income exceeding $200,000 ($300,000 jointly with a spouse) in each of the two most recent years, with a reasonable expectation of the same for the current year. The definition also includes licensed investment professionals (Series 7, 65, or 82) and certain entities.

When will the fund close and when do I receive distributions?

The fund targets a 5–7 year investment horizon. Cash distributions from farm rental income and operational proceeds are paid quarterly once the fund has deployed capital and farms are producing. The offering memorandum will contain the specific projected distribution timeline for this fund.

Is my investment backed by physical farmland?

Yes. The fund acquires title to productive U.S. farmland. Each investor's interest represents a proportional claim on a portfolio of physical land assets — not derivative instruments or paper farmland products. The underlying assets are real, producing agricultural land in the United States.

Can I exit my investment before the fund term ends?

This is a private placement with no guaranteed secondary market. Early exits are subject to the terms set out in the Private Placement Memorandum, which may include transfer restrictions or a right-of-first-refusal mechanism. Investors should treat this as an illiquid investment intended to be held for the full fund term.

Who manages the fund and what is Green Earth Group N.V.?

First Mover Fund is an initiative of Green Earth Group N.V., a European investment group with roots going back to 1957. Green Earth Group has a long history of managing real asset portfolios for investors across Europe. The Farmland America Fund is managed by First Mover's U.S. investment team in partnership with Green Earth Group's institutional infrastructure, governance standards, and investor reporting framework.
