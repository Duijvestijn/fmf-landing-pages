The land advantage

Two income streams from one investment

Agricultural land is the only asset class that generates both traditional cash income and environmental value simultaneously — and both are growing fast.

Agricultural yield

Our partner farms produce high-value crops — coffee, cocoa, specialty grains — generating rental income that flows directly to investors every quarter.

  • Long-term leases with proven farm operators
  • Crop insurance and diversified commodity exposure
  • 8% annual cash yield, paid quarterly
  • Rising food prices protect purchasing power

Environmental credits

Regenerative land management generates carbon credits, biodiversity units, and water rights — a booming market that adds a second income layer on top of crop income.

  • Carbon sequestration — certified credits sold annually
  • Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG) units
  • Voluntary carbon market demand surging
  • Regulatory tailwinds driving credit prices higher
Why land with FMF

The world's oldest asset class, reinvented

Land doesn't depreciate, can't be manufactured, and has fed civilisations for millennia. We've made it investable for everyone — starting with the U.S. market.

Finite supply, infinite demand

U.S. farmland acreage is shrinking every year through development and degradation. Meanwhile global food demand rises relentlessly — making supply constraints permanent.

50-year inflation hedge

U.S. farmland has outpaced inflation by an average of 4–6% annually over 50 years. When the cost of living rises, so does the land that produces it.

Regenerative value creation

Land we farm regeneratively becomes more fertile and more biodiverse over time. You don't just hold an asset — you actively improve it while earning from it.

Fully managed — zero admin

We handle all farm operations, environmental certifications, credit sales, tenant management, and compliance. You own the economics; we run the land.

ESG returns — real impact

Every project sequesters carbon, restores biodiversity, and improves water quality. Your investment generates financial returns and measurable environmental impact.

Transparent reporting

As a listed company, our financials are public. Your investor dashboard shows farm outputs, credit generation, and quarterly distributions in real time.
