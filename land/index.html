The land advantage

Two income streams from one investment

Agricultural land is the only asset class generating both traditional cash income and environmental value simultaneously — and both are growing.

Agricultural yield

Our partner farms produce high-value crops generating rental income that flows directly to investors every quarter — the same model Dutch land funds have used for 67 years.

  • Long-term leases with proven American farm operators
  • Crop insurance and diversified commodity exposure
  • 8% annual cash yield, paid quarterly to your account
  • Rising food prices protect your purchasing power

Environmental credits

Regenerative land management generates carbon credits, biodiversity units, and water rights — a booming market adding a second income layer on top of crop income.

  • Carbon sequestration — certified credits sold annually
  • Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG) units — growing fast
  • Voluntary carbon market demand surging globally
  • Regulatory tailwinds driving credit prices higher
