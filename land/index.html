The land advantage

Two income streams from one investment

Agricultural land is the only asset class that generates both traditional income and environmental value simultaneously — and both are growing.

Agricultural yield

Our partner farms produce high-value crops — coffee, cocoa, specialty grains — generating consistent rental income that flows directly to investors quarterly.

  • Crop insurance and diversified commodity exposure
  • 8% annual cash yield paid quarterly

Environmental credits

Regenerative land management generates carbon credits, biodiversity credits, and water rights — a rapidly growing market that adds a second income layer on top of crop yields.

  • Carbon sequestration certified credits
  • Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG) units
  • Voluntary carbon market sales
  • Growing regulatory demand driving credit prices up
Why land with FMF

The world's oldest asset class, reinvented

Land doesn't depreciate, can't be manufactured, and has fed civilisations for millennia. We've made it investable for everyone.

Finite supply, infinite demand

The world's farmable land shrinks every year through urbanisation and degradation. Meanwhile global food demand rises. Supply constraints drive land value permanently upward.

Proven inflation hedge

Over 50 years, farmland has outpaced inflation by an average of 4–6% annually. When the cost of living rises, so does the value of the land producing it.

Regenerative upside

Land we farm regeneratively becomes more fertile, more biodiverse, and more valuable over time. You don't just hold an asset — you improve it while you hold it.

Fully managed

First Mover Fund handles all farm operations, environmental certifications, credit sales, and compliance. You own the land economics; we manage the land.

ESG-aligned returns

Every project actively restores ecosystems, sequesters carbon, and improves biodiversity. Your investment generates financial returns and measurable environmental impact.

Real-time reporting

Track your investment performance, farm outputs, carbon credit generation, and quarterly distributions through your investor dashboard. Full transparency, always.
