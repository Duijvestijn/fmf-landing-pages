Return structure

Two income streams, one investment

Real estate investments generate returns in multiple ways. We optimise for both.

  • Rental yield

    Net rental income distributed as 8% annual cash yield, paid quarterly to your account.

  • Capital appreciation

    Property value growth over the investment horizon, realised at exit and returned to investors.

  • Value-add upside

    Active asset management — refurbishments, rezoning, lease optimisation — creates additional above-market returns.

  • Inflation protection

    Real estate is a proven inflation hedge. As prices rise, both rental income and property values typically rise with them.
